Exam Scoring Pattern

Our exams are scored based on a weighted system:

  • Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs): 40% (10 points each)
  • True/False Questions: 20% (10 points each)
  • Subjective Questions: 30% (15 points each)
  • File Upload: 10% (10 points)

Scores are aggregated and converted to a percentage. A minimum of 60% is required to pass.
