Examinations at Aurora Academy

Aurora Academy offers examinations in a variety of subjects, including English, Math, Information Technology (IT), History, Geography, Psychology, and more. These exams are conducted twice a year, in September and March, to assess students' knowledge and skills. The assessments include written tests, practical tasks, and project-based evaluations, designed to provide a comprehensive measure of academic progress.
